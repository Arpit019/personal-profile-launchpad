## The AI Wrapper Trap

Currently, thousands of startups and enterprise product teams are building thin UI wrappers over OpenAI's APIs. They plug a prompt into GPT-4, put a clean React frontend on it, and call it an "AI Product."

This is not a sustainable product strategy. Why? Because OpenAI's next minor update will likely render your wrapper entirely obsolete. If your entire value proposition is simply routing text to an LLM, you have zero defensive moats.

### Building Defensive Moats in AI

To survive the exponential curve of Generative AI, you must build products where the AI is an *accelerant*, not the foundation.

### Proprietary Data is King

In a world where intelligence (LLMs) is becoming commoditized, data is the only remaining differentiator. 

If you are a Product Manager, your priority should be restructuring your data pipelines to be LLM-friendly. Build robust vector databases (like Pinecone) based on your users' historical behaviors, proprietary documents, and niche industry data.

When you feed an LLM your deeply proprietary data via Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), you stop being an "AI Wrapper" and start being an "Intelligent System of Record." That is how you build a billion-dollar product in 2026.
